Konsumentor administration interface

The Konsumentor database consists of four main areas

  • Article and product listings
  • Brands and owner listings
  • Ingredients listings
  • Reviews connected to items in the listings mentioned above
By using the tabs you can navigate between theese areas and add information.

Products, brands and owners

All articles are unique packages of specific products, sold under a brand. As an example, Coca Cola 1L is an article, while Coca Cola is a product sold under the brand Coca Cola, the same goes for Coca Cola Light and Coca Cola Cherry, which are different products from the same brand. Fanta and Sprite are beverages with different brands, but they are also manufactured by the same owner, The Coca Cola Company.
All articles has a unique GTIN number which can be found encoded in an bar code on its package.

This system enables organisations to review not only specific products or brands, but also international conglomerates. For example all the products under the Axe brand can be blacklisted toghether with Pea Soup and toothpaste manufactured by the owner Unilever because of under-paid farmers growing coconuts in South America which are used in Ice Cream sold under yet another brand from Unilever.
